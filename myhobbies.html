Travel
All that is gold does not glitter. Not all those who wander are lost.
- J.R.R. Tolkien

I love to travel. I love learning about new places and people, discovering different cultures. And of course, eating. Travel allows me to do all that and more.

My first experience out of the country was Quebec City in Quebec, CANADA. Though it may not seem very far from Minnesota, it was a great international expereince. It is one of my favorite places I have visited, and I hope to go visit it again one day.
